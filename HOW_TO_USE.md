# How to Use AEGIS — Your Local AI Guardian

Welcome to the **AEGIS AI Guardian Operating Layer**. This guide will help you navigate the app, understand how the AI model installs itself, and how to stay protected.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. The Automatic AI Setup
When you first open AEGIS, the **Gemma 3N** model (approx. 1.5GB) will begin downloading and initializing automatically in the background.
- **Progress Tracking**: You can view the loading status at the top of the **AI Assistant** screen.
- **Prerequisites**: Ensure you have at least **2GB of free space** and a stable internet connection for the initial setup. Once loaded, AEGIS works **100% offline**.

### 2. Granting Permissions (CRITICAL)
For AEGIS to protect you, it needs to see what's happening on your device. Follow the prompts to enable:
- **Notification Listener**: Allows the **Notification Guardian** to detect scams in WhatsApp, SMS, and Telegram.
- **Accessibility Service**: Enables the **Screen Guardian** to warn you about malicious links or invasive permission requests in real-time.
- **Overlay Permission**: Required to show the **AEGIS Threat Alert** over other apps.

---

## 🗺 App Navigation

### **1. Dashboard (The Command Center)**
- **Safety Score**: Your overall digital health. Click it to see detailed scores for Privacy, Scam Protection, and more.
- **Run Guardian Scan**: Tap this to perform a deep, AI-powered audit of your installed apps and system settings.
- **Recent Alerts**: A quick list of the latest threats AEGIS has blocked or flagged.

### **2. AI Assistant (Your Security Partner)**
- Tap the **SmartToy** icon to chat with the Gemma-powered assistant.
- Ask questions like: *"Is this WhatsApp message a scam?"* or *"Should I allow this app to access my contacts?"*
- The assistant analyzes your **recent device history** to give context-aware advice.

### **3. Privacy Center**
- **Camera & Mic Lock**: Toggle these to prevent unauthorized background access.
- **Permission Advisor**: See real-time AI recommendations when an app asks for sensitive data.
- **Identity Leak Check**: Run scans to see if your email or phone has been leaked in data breaches.

### **4. Security Vault**
- **Hardware Encryption**: Store your most sensitive IDs and documents.
- **Biometric Unlock**: Access your vault using your fingerprint or FaceID.

---

## 🛡 How Protection Works

### **The Notification Guardian**
When a suspicious message arrives (e.g., a "bank" asking for an OTP), AEGIS intercepts it, Gemma analyzes the **last 20 messages** for context, and a **Threat Overlay** appears if the risk is high.

### **The Link Guardian**
If you are about to click a deceptive link (like `paypa1-secure.xyz`), the Screen Guardian will pop up an alert explaining the brand impersonation before you even open the browser.

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting
- **AI not responding?** Check the loading bar in the Assistant screen. The first load can take 1nd-2 minutes depending on your device speed.
- **No alerts appearing?** Ensure "Display over other apps" is enabled in your Android System Settings for AEGIS.
